Fabric Expansion Joint Bellows

  • Fabric Expansion Joints have distinct advantages as they can accommodate relatively large movements and even some misalignment of flanges. They are also lightweight, easy to install and with the addition of internal insulation bolsters have good acoustic properties. Fabric expansion joints can be supplied fully assembled and fitted to support steelwork ready for insertion into ductwork. For retrofit applications, we can supply as factory joined or open for on-site joining where access is limited.
  • Fabric Expansion Joints are in use in Power Plants, Gas Turbine installations, Offshore installations, Cement works, the Chemical Industry and many others. Rex fabric expansion joints are available in virtually any size and shape, from a range of proven standard constructions or designed specifically for your application. The combined temperature, pressure and media are all decisive factors in the selection of fabric expansion joints.
  • Rex has always believed in the importance of Design criteria, selection of correct raw materials, manufacturing and installation of the Fabric Expansion Joints (bellows).

Compressed Non Asbestos Jointing Sheet

Compressed Non-Asbestos Jointing Sheet fills the space between two or more matting surfaces, generally to prevent leakage from or into the joint objects while under compression. Rex Jointing sheets conforms the property requirements of international standards & has many desirable properties to withstand high compressive loads. Carbon - Graphite Packing : RI - 36

REX INDUSTRIES established in 1963 has since solved several leakage hazards for various industrial applications. REX believes is TPM (Total Productivity Maintenance).

"REX" Carbon - Graphite Packing (RI - 36) is manufactured by careful selection of graphite fibres and treating them with a CARBON solute which provides the packing the High Temperature Resistance which is its MAJOR CHARACTERISTIC.

"REX" Carbon - Graphite Packing (RI - 36) is guaranteed to provide virtually ZERO LEAKAGE and will play a major role in enhancing TPM activities." REX" Carbon - Graphite Packing ( RI - 36) is designed to be used in ROTATORY as well as STATIC equipments.
