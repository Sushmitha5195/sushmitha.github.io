VEEDOL INDIA

Tide Water Oil Co. India Ltd. is one of the leading manufacturers and marketers of quality lubricants in India. It has been catering to the automotive and industrial segments since 1928. Tide Water Oil Co. (India) Ltd. is a part of the multi divisional Andrew Yule group and its registered office is at Kolkata, West Bengal. We own and market Veedol brand in India and abroad.

Its repertoire of automotive products includes high performance engine oils for HCVs, buses, tractors, Medium / Light commercial vehicles, passenger cars, and two / three wheelers. It also produces gear oils, transmission oils, coolants, and greases for all automotive applications. The entire product range offers appropriate choices for various applications and performance levels keeping pace with OEM’s latest technical requirements. For industrial application, it manufactures various types of conventional and speciality application oils, greases, and customised products to meet typical application requirement Tide Water Oil has also got tie-ups for genuine oils with a number of renowned OEMs in both automotive and industrial equipment segments.
