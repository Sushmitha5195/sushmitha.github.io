About us

Best Services To The Industries

Kay Cee Enterprises was established in the year 2003 in Mangalore by K Channayya a qualified Mechanical Engineer. Currently we stand as one of the leading industrial suppliers in the coastal belt of Karnataka with 3 branches spread across Mangalore and Udupi.

Kay Cee Enterprises is a professionally managed organisation majorly engaged in supplying wide range of industrial products. With a team of qualified engineers , providing solutions for all kinds of engineering problems and services round the clock has been our main motto. Maintaining a strong hold in the market by selling quality products and providing customer satisfaction over the years has helped us grow leaps and bounds.
