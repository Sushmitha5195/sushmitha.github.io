CAROL PETROLEUM

Under the leadership of Mr. Paresh R. Parekh, Carol Petroleum has been making products that are customised as per the customer's needs for over two decades. With this single driving factor, we have been one of the companies at the forefront of the development of petroleum and petrochemical equipment and operating standards.Each of our products are designed to offer long oil life, minimal recurring cost and less consumption for our clients, making it a leader in innovative solutions that meet global standards of business etiquette.

We cater to a wide range of industrial lubricant requirements across the industries such as automobile, engineering, steel, cement, sugar, machining etc. in India and Overseas. These lubes work under extreme pressure conditions and have anti-wear additives that assure quick, accurate, and high quality lubrication. There is minimum tool wear and the oils do not leave residues. Even at very high temperatures, your machines function efficiently. We also have products tailor made for different techno-commercial requirements of a particular customer.
